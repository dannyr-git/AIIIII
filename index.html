Yudkowskian Existential Risk Index (YERI) Calculation

The AIIIII has devised an algorithm to control advanced artificial intelligence (AI) development, named the 'Yudkowskian Existential Risk Index' (YERI). The YERI is determined using the following formula:

Yudkowskian_Existential_Risk_Index = log10[(Quantity_of_State_of_Art_GPUs * Revolutionary_Update_Frequency) / (Yudkowskian_Doomerism_Factor * Progress_towards_O_N)]

Mathematical notation:

YERI = log10[(QSoAGPUs * RUF) / (YDF * PoON)]

Where:

  • Quantity_of_State_of_Art_GPUs (QSoAGPUs): The number of top-tier Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) owned, rented, or otherwise accessed by the entity.
  • Revolutionary_Update_Frequency (RUF): The total count of transformative capabilities implemented and released within the preceding fiscal year, excluding minor tweaks, bug fixes, or aesthetic enhancements.
  • Yudkowskian_Doomerism_Factor (YDF): The frequency of direct mentions of the entity in question by renowned AI researcher Eliezer Yudkowsky on public platforms (particularly Twitter) within the past twelve months.
  • Progress_towards_O_N (PoON): A measure of the entity's progress towards approaching a time complexity of O(N) for their Large Language Model's attention mechanisms, where N represents the number of attention heads.
