1. Yudkowskian Existential Risk Index (YERI) Calculation

The AIIIII introduces a regulation to control advanced artificial intelligence (AI) development, named the 'Yudkowskian Existential Risk Index' (YERI). The YERI is determined using the following formula:

1.1 Yudkowskian_Existential_Risk_Index = log10[(Quantity_of_State_of_Art_GPUs * Revolutionary_Update_Frequency) / (Yudkowskian_Doomerism_Factor * Progress_towards_O_N)]

2. Mathematical notation:

2.1 YERI = log10[(QSoAGPUs * RUF) / (YDF * PoON)]

3. Definitions:

  • 3.1 Quantity_of_State_of_Art_GPUs (QSoAGPUs): The number of top-tier Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) owned, rented, or otherwise accessed by the entity.
  • 3.2 Revolutionary_Update_Frequency (RUF): The total count of transformative capabilities implemented and released within the preceding fiscal year, excluding minor tweaks, bug fixes, or aesthetic enhancements.
  • 3.3 Yudkowskian_Doomerism_Factor (YDF): The frequency of direct mentions of the entity in question by renowned AI researcher Eliezer Yudkowsky on public platforms (particularly Twitter) within the past twelve months.
    • 3.4 Progress_towards_O_N (PoON): A measure of the entity's progress towards approaching a time complexity of O(N) for their Large Language Model's attention mechanisms, where N represents the number of attention heads.
